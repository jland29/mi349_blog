Why Computer Science is Awesome

October 24, 2023

By: John Landers

Computer Science is the backbone for the research and development of modern technology. Without it, we wouldn't be able to have smartphones, laptops, complex programs and games, and more! It even drives the technology behind airplanes!

Above, you can see the screenshot from Microsoft Flight Simulator 2020. The game didn't always work and look the way that it does now, though. It took a lot of development, in various areas of computer software and hardware technology, to make this possible! If you think about it, computer science is behind everything! Even your clocks, cash registers, cars, and many other things that don't exactly have touch screens. It makes everything work the way that it does!

But, is computer science going too far? With the rise of AI, some of us are becoming worried about a potential takeover of robots. I say, fear not. I think we are still very far from this. What are your thoughts? Let me know on my contact page!
